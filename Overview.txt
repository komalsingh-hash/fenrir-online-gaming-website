Overview
Metro: Last Departure is an animated online gaming website created to promote a single-player survival-exploration game set within a metropolitan transit network during a zombie outbreak. Following a mysterious experimental gas leak from a sealed research facility, players must navigate abandoned stations, contaminated tunnels, and dangerous transit routes while uncovering the truth behind the outbreak and the metro system's involvement. The landing page serves as the game's pre-launch marketing platform, introducing visitors to the game's storyline, character classes, survival mechanics, interactive world, and multiple endings through immersive visuals, animations, and interactive content. Designed with a dark metro-inspired aesthetic and responsive user experience, the website aims to build anticipation and encourage player engagement ahead of the game's release.
