Scope of Work
The scope of this project is to design and develop a fully responsive, animated single-page online gaming website for Metro: Last Departure. The website will serve as the primary pre-launch marketing platform for the game, introducing visitors to its storyline, world, unlockable character classes, survival mechanics, and multiple endings through interactive content and immersive visual design. The landing page will include eight sections: Hero, Gameplay Features, Interactive Metro Map, Survivor Roster, Story Teaser, Survival Mechanics, Endings Preview, and CTA Footer. The project includes the implementation of glitch effects, hover interactions, card transitions, scroll-triggered reveals, route progression animations, and accessibility-focused motion controls. Deliverables include organized front-end source files, responsive layouts for desktop and mobile devices, supporting documentation, and project assets. The scope is limited to front-end website development and does not include backend functionality, user authentication, databases, live data integration, leaderboards, social features, multiplayer systems, or actual game development.
